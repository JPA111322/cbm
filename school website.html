Welcome to BSHM Website

The Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management is a ladderized program that prepares students to have employable skills/competencies at every academic year level for them to be competitive in the hospitality industry locally and globally.

There are so many job opportunities, in hotels, cruises, and restaurants. It is a very fulfilling career because you get to be happy by serving others.

The salary level is average locally, but when you're abroad, it is pretty high. Hospitality Management prepares you for a wide variety of careers in different sectors of hospitality, enables you to transform the industry with innovative ideas, and offers you exciting global opportunities.
